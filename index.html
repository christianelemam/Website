Since November 2024, I am a postdoc at the University of Torino under the supervision of Andrea Seppi.
Before that, I was a postdoc at the University of Luxembourg and did my PhD at the University of Pavia.

My main research interests lie in geometric structures on manifolds and equivariant immersions in symmetric spaces. More broadly, I'm interested in (higher) Teichmüller theory and Differential Geometry.
